# FetchUp Conversion Engine: The Reply Playbook (v1.0)
**Last Updated: 2026-05-15**

## The Goal
To transition a "Thanks for the heads up!" comment into an off-platform conversation (DM/Email) and eventually a paid subscription.

---

## Phase 1: The "Auditor's Pivot"
*When they reply to your initial 404 alert.*

### Archetype A: The Polite Creator ("Thanks so much! Fixed it.")
**Goal:** Challenge their manual process.
- **The Reply:** "No worries! It's crazy how often those sneak up on you. Do you actually have a system to track these or do you just find them by luck when someone comments?"

### Archetype B: The Skeptical Creator ("Works for me / Are you sure?")
**Goal:** Prove the "Evidence-First" authority.
- **The Reply:** "Ah, weird. I'm seeing a 404 on my end (tried a few times). Sometimes these links break only in certain regions or when redirects get stripped. I actually built a scanner for my own channel to find these—want me to run a full check on your last 10 vids for free to be sure?"

### Archetype C: The Busy Creator ("Thanks! I'll look into it later.")
**Goal:** Frictionless Value.
- **The Reply:** "Totally get it—description audits are a mission. Tell you what, I’ll run a quick scan on your recent uploads and DM you a list of any others I find so you don't have to hunt them down manually. What's the best way to reach you?"

---

## Phase 2: The "Irrresistible Gift" (Off-Platform)
*When they agree to the scan or ask 'How do you do that?'*

**The Goal:** Get them to see the FetchUp Dashboard / Report.

- **Message:** "Just ran the scan on your last 10 videos. Found [X] more dead links—looks like the [Product A] and [Product B] ones are costing you about $[Y] in commissions per month. I put the full report here: [Link to FetchUp or Screenshot]"

---

## Phase 3: The "Soft Close"
*When they see the leakage.*

- **The Message:** "It’s a headache to keep these alive manually. If you want, you can use the monitor I built—it checks your links 24/7 so you never have to worry about it. I’m letting a few creators in for $15/mo while I'm in stealth. Interested in a pro account?"

---

## The "Spam-Free" Rules
1. **Never Link First:** Wait for a response before mentioning FetchUp.
2. **Lead with ROI:** Always mention the *Views* or *Estimated Loss*.
3. **Be a Peer:** Use "I built this for my own channel" to build trust.
