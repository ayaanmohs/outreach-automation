# FetchUp Master Context: The Digital Brain

## Purpose
This folder is the "thinking engine" behind FetchUp. It stores the project's soul, the founder's vision, and the strategic roadmap. When switching between AI agents or starting new sessions, this is the first place to read to understand the full context.

## Navigation Map

### 1. [Founder Context](./founder_context/README.md)
- **Who**: Context on the founder (preferences, work style, long-term goals).
- **Voice**: How the brand and the founder communicate.

### 2. [Product Context](./product_context/README.md)
- **The Vision**: Why FetchUp exists (solving "Silent Monetization Leakage").
- **The Tech**: Official YouTube API, Express Backend, Tailwind Frontend, PostHog/GA4 Analytics.
- **Stealth MVP**: The current 3-page architecture (Landing, Onboarding, Dashboard).

### 3. [Outreach & Growth](./outreach_context/README.md)
- **Strategy**: Manual high-intent outreach vs. the upcoming Lead Scraper automation.
- **Snapshot**: [Latest Strategy & Voice](./outreach_context/STRATEGY_SNAPSHOT.md) - **READ THIS FIRST FOR OUTREACH TASKS.**
- **Leads**: Target criteria (10k-100k subs, productivity/tech niche).

### 4. [Decision Log](./decision_log/README.md)
- **Log**: A chronological record of why we made certain choices (e.g., Why we chose Koyeb, why we use a `www` redirect).

---

## Instructions for AI Agents
1. **Read First**: Always read the `MASTER_CONTEXT.md` and the `README.md` in each subfolder before making architectural or strategic suggestions.
2. **Respect the Vision**: Decisions should align with the "Stealth MVP" philosophy—speed, precision, and high-quality UX over bloated features.
3. **Update the Brain**: When a major decision is made or a strategy changes, update the relevant file in this folder immediately.
