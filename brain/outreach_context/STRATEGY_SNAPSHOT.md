# FetchUp Strategy Snapshot (Last Updated: 2026-05-21)

## 1. Core Identity & Voice
- **Persona:** World-class founder, god-tier marketer.
- **Voice:** Peer-to-peer, scrappy, lowercase, non-professional.
- **Philosophy:** Precision > Volume. Problem-Finding > Lead-Scraping.

## 2. Winning Outreach Formula (v3.2+)
- **Hook:** "is it just me or everyone..." or "wanted to grab the [product] but..."
- **The Pain:** Focus on "Lost Sales" and "Commission Leakage."
- **The Words:** Use "dead," "acting up," "looping." Avoid "404" or "Systemised."
- **The Goal:** Move from Comment -> Reply -> "Automated Audit" offer.

## 3. High-Value Targeting
- **Niches:** Physical Gear (Photography, EDC, Home Office, Racing Rigs).
- **Sweet Spot:** 1k - 250k subscribers.
- **Automation:** Scraper v4.0 (Data Sniper) provides: Video Link, Broken Product, Channel Handle.

## 4. Team Structure
- **Technical Lead:** Developer (Runs scraper, handles deep conversion).
- **Operations Lead:** Brother (Manual copy-paste, account warming, initial replies).
- **Target:** 10 "Evidence" comments per day.

## 5. Technical Stack
- **Lead Discovery:** `scraper.py` (Official YT API).
- **Link Auditing:** `site_checker.py` (Brute force URL finder).
- **Conversion:** FetchUp MVP (Revenue Leak Calculator).
