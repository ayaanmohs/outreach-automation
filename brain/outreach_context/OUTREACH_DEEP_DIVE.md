# FetchUp Outreach & Product Deep-Dive

## 1. Product Brief: What is FetchUp?
FetchUp is a "Revenue Recovery" tool for professional YouTube creators. 
- **The Problem**: Creators have hundreds of videos. Over time, affiliate links in descriptions break (404s), domains expire, or affiliate parameters are stripped during redirects. This is "Silent Monetization Leakage."
- **The Solution**: A high-precision scanner that audits every link in a video description.
- **The "Aha!" Moment**: The **Revenue Leak Calculator**. We don't just say "your link is broken"; we say "this broken link is costing you approximately $X,XXX based on your views."
- **Current Tech**: Uses the Official YouTube API for 100% reliability. Conservative logic (only 404 is "Broken") to ensure high credibility.

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
We are NOT looking for every YouTuber. We are looking for "Professional Inventory Holders."

### **The "Sweet Spot" Lead**
- **Channel Size**: 10k to 250k subscribers. (Large enough to have traffic, small enough to care about $50-$200/mo leaks).
- **Niche**: Productivity, Tech, Gear Reviews, "Top 10" lists, Software Tutorials (Obsidian, Notion, Apple ecosystem).
- **Content Type**: "Alternative" videos (e.g., "Top 5 MacBook Accessories") where 5-10 external links are used.
- **The Trigger**: A video older than 6 months that is still getting "evergreen" views.

## 3. The Outreach Failure Audit (150 Comments, 0 Visitors)
- **The Method**: Opening with a question to "initiate conversation."
- **Why it failed**: 
    1. **Friction**: You are asking the creator to do work (answer a question) before giving value.
    2. **Pattern Recognition**: Creators are bombarded with "Great video! Can I ask a question?" which usually leads to a sales pitch. It looks like a bot.
    3. **Lack of Proof**: You didn't show them the money they are losing *immediately*.

## 4. The New Strategy: "The Evidence-First Open"
Instead of asking a question, we lead with **Specific Evidence**.
- **The Goal**: To be the "helpful auditor," not the "salesman."
- **The Hook**: "Hey [Name], just a heads up—the [Product] link in this description is currently 404ing. Since this video gets ~[X] views a month, you're likely losing a bit of revenue there. Fixed it yet?"

## 5. Conversation Starters (The "Relatability" Angle)
- **Question for the other agent**: "How can we frame the 'Broken Link' find as a casual favor rather than a cold pitch?"
- **Keywords for targeting**: "Evergreen," "Passive Income," "Affiliate Management," "Description Audit."
